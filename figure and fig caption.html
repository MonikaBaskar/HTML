Brihadeeshwara Temple

The Brihadeshwara temple was built in 1010 AD by the Chola King. It is situated in Thanjavur, Tamil Nadu. The famous deity in the temple is of Lord Shiva in a dancing pose which is known as Nataraj. It was the first temple to be made of granite.

The Brihadeshwara Temple at Thanjavur was built by Rajaraja I in 1009 CE for worshipping Lord Shiva. Many of his achievements are inscribed here. It is the best example of Chola architecture.
